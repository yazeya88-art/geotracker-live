The Process

How it works.

Four steps, thirty seconds, five language models. No installation, no credit card, no waiting.

01

Submit your details

Enter your business domain, name, industry, and city. We support 16 service categories from dental to legal to HVAC.

02

We query 5 AI models

ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, and Grok are asked the same five industry-specific questions your real customers ask — in parallel.

03

Each response is scored

We detect whether you're named, where you rank, and whether the AI gave a way to contact you. Each cell becomes green, yellow, or red.

04

You get your Ghost Score

A single 0–100 score, a 5×5 visibility matrix, gap analysis, and a path to fix what's broken. All in under 30 seconds.
